pilau ingredients

  • bassmatt rice
  • pilau masala
  • meat
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • tomato paste

procedure

  1. wash the rice
  2. boil the meat for an hour
  3. crush your ginger and garlic
  4. fry your onions,garlic and ginger
  5. add meat and rice
  6. add tomato paste and stir
  7. cover your pot for 30 minutes to cook evenly
